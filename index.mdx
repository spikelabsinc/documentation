## The Vision

We believe AI should be more than a tool. It should be a presence. Not a simulacrum pretending to be human, but something new: an entity that perceives, understands, and responds with genuine awareness of the moment.

Spike gives you the building blocks to create AI companions that exist fully in conversation. They see the user's expressions, hear the nuance in their voice, speak with authentic personality, and remember what matters.

## The Flow

A user speaks. Your AI companion perceives. Not just the words, but the furrowed brow, the hesitation, the smile breaking through. It draws on everything it knows: the personality you've crafted, the knowledge you've provided, the memories of this particular user. A response forms. Not retrieved, but genuinely composed for this moment. It speaks with its own voice, its face animated with appropriate emotion.

All of this happens in under a second. The conversation feels natural because it *is* natural. Two entities present with each other, communicating through the full bandwidth of human expression.
